# "CAPTCHA #203" by Benka — Mixed Media on Canvas, 2023

![CAPTCHA #203 by Benka — mixed media canvas featuring a single CAPTCHA character against a field of dark gestural marks, abstract street art, raw layered paint surface, human vs machine tension](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/635e4fa10b918d8093148df1/65a8273377868f16cf2485e5_327016551.jpg)

## About This Work

"CAPTCHA #203" (180 × 140 cm) presents a single verification character confronting the viewer head-on. The surrounding field is dense with gesture — accumulated strokes, scraped surfaces, and overlapping media that record the physical labour of painting. Against this restless ground, the character reads almost as an apparition: partly legible, partly consumed by its own context.

This canvas is part of the CAPTCHA series through which [Benka](/artists/benka) interrogates the boundary between human and algorithmic recognition. Each numbered work takes the anti-bot verification test as its subject, transforming a disposable digital prompt into a sustained painterly inquiry. The series draws on [Benka](/artists/benka)'s clinical background — his research into smartphone addiction gives the work a precise theoretical grounding that distinguishes it from purely formal abstraction.

"CAPTCHA #203" is a focused, intimate entry in the series. The 180 × 140 cm format is well-suited to residential and institutional interiors alike. Collectors interested in the intersection of technology critique and painterly tradition will find this work particularly resonant. [Benka](/artists/benka)'s international exhibition record across Europe and the US supports sustained collector interest.
